## About me

I enjoy building at the intersection of creativity, technology, visuals, and people.

I am naturally reflective, explorative, and idea-driven. I do not just want to make things that work. I want to create things that feel meaningful, useful, and alive.

With a background in public relations and organizational work, I care deeply about communication, empathy, collaboration, and building experiences that make people feel understood.

## How I work best

- I like to understand the big picture first, then break it into clear small steps.
- I learn best by listening, watching examples, trying them, modifying them, and turning them into mini projects.
- I am often at my most creative during quiet late-night sessions with music in the background.
- I care about finishing, not just starting.

My working principle:

> Capture ideas -> choose priorities -> build fast -> finish -> document.
